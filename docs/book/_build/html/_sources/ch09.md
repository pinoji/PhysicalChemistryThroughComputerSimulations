## Symmetry operation

Symmetry operation is the permutation of atoms that leaves the molecule
unchanged. We look at rotation $(C_n)$ , reflection $(\sigma)$,
inversion$(i)$, inproper rotation $(S_n)$ and identity$(E)$.

Symmetry operation E does not do anything. This smily face does not have
any symmetry operation that bring back the original figure. Operation
$C_2$ is the rotation around rotational axis ($\perp$ to this paper).
Note that $C_2$ restores the original figure. Reflection operation is
the reflection with respect to mirror plane (dashed plane). It also
restore the original figure. Inversion operation $i$ is the operation
that move any points to the other side of the origin (black dot).
Finally, the improper rotation $S_6$ rotate any points by $60^o$ and
reflect with respect to $\sigma$ plane (plane between 2 hexagram). Note
that mirror plane is $\perp$ to the rotational axis.\

## Group Theory

Group theory is a topic of pure mathematics.\
Basically,\
A group is a of transformation that satisfy the following:\

<hr/>
  1⃝   collection include \"identity\" $= I$ (do nothing)
      
  2⃝   for every transformation $R$, there is an \"inverse\" transformation $R^{-1}$ such that
      $RR^{-1} = R^{-1}R = I$
      
  3⃝   any combination of transformation in the collection is also in the collection
      $RR' = R'' \rightarrow \text{ also in the set}$
      
  4⃝   associative
      $(RR')R'' = R(R'R'')$
<hr/>

##  Representaion Theory

It is a branch of mathematics. A representation makes and abstract
algebraic object more concrete by describing its element by matrices.\

$\underbrace{\text{group elements}}_{\text{sym operation}}$ can be

[Note]{.underline}\
$3 \times 3$ matrix is not the only representation of the $C_{2v}$ point
